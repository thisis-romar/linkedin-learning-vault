They didn't know whom to ask when they needed help or guidance, and so they quit, the solution? Different color shirts for the shift managers, employee retention, shot up double digits. I am a huge advocate for all that AI can achieve for our organizations and for society, but it will never reach its potential through the vehicle of use cases, in the context of strategy, use cases are the friend of engineering and we should keep using in that context.
