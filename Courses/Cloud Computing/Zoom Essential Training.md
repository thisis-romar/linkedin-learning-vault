# Zoom Essential Training

> Collaborate more seamlessly using Zoom, the popular video conferencing tool. In this course, staff instructor Garrick Chow shows how to schedule, moderate, and participate in Zoom meetings and webinars. Garrick begins with how to set up and access your Zoom account and adjust settings and filters. Next, he guides you through setting up and initiating a meeting, as well as joining a meeting someone
