# Slack Essential Training

> As a communication and collaboration tool, Slack is powerful yet fun to use. In this course, instructor Gini von Courter begins by showing how to log into an existing team. She shows how to customize your profile and start writing and replying to messages in Slack. Gini covers how to upload, share, and comment on files. Then she goes into how to search for content, work with channels, and add apps
