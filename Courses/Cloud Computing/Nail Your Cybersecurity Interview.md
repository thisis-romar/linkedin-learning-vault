> **[2:23](https://www.linkedin.com/learning/nail-your-cybersecurity-interview/the-three-common-types-of-cybersecurity-interviews?u=76281980&t=143)** Sure this person is actually working in the role day-to-day, but management has already assessed your technical capabilities.
