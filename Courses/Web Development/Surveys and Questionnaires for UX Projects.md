# Surveys and Questionnaires for UX Projects

> Surveys are one of the most frequently used—and abused—methods of getting information from a group of people. Done well, they are illuminating. Done poorly, they are almost useless. If you are conducting marketing or usability research, or just trying to learn more about your customers, you should know how to conduct an effective survey or questionnaire. In this course, Chris Nodder provides tips
