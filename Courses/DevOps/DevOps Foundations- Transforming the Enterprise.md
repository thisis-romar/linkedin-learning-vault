# DevOps Foundations: Transforming the Enterprise

> As your organization evolves, your IT practices can't remain stagnant. Growing organizations are faced with a variety of new and unique concerns: legacy programs, multiple vendors, and more. In this course, learn how to adopt and scale DevOps—a set of practices you may already be familiar with—in an enterprise environment. Instructor Mirco Hering explains what makes enterprise-level DevOps unique
