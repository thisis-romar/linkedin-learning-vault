# Copilot in Word: Create and Refine Documents with AI

> Discover the power of AI in Microsoft Word with Copilot, your new writing assistant. Leverage Copilot to create new documents, refine existing ones, and distill key points from complex material. This course provides a wide range of prompt examples you can use to inspire and improve your writing, and better understand long or complex documents.
