Discussions are meant for broader conversations like design decisions, long-term planning, or general questions. Discussions empower a project's maintainers, contributors, and visitors to gather and address topics in a central location without the need for a third-party tool. Like issues, they can be easily organized via tagging system by category type, similar to labels and issues, making it easier to find relevant topics. Discussions are typically less focused on individual tasks or bugs, and more on community interaction and decision making.
