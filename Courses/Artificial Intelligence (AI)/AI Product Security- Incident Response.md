> **[1:32](https://www.linkedin.com/learning/ai-product-security-incident-response/incident-reporting-obligations?u=76281980&t=92)** In this context, the category serious means malfunctioning, leading to death, or serious damage to health, serious and irreversible disruption of the management and operation of critical infrastructure or infringements of obligations under European Union law intended to protect fundamental rights, or serious damage to property or the environment.

> **[2:49](https://www.linkedin.com/learning/ai-product-security-incident-response/incident-reporting-obligations?u=76281980&t=169)** In this context, it requires organizations which deploy AI models to report any significant incidents or model failures to developers in order to ensure improvements can be made to the model.
