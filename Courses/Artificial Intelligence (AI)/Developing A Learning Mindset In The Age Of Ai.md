Luckily, you have a second set of skills, flex skills, which are transferable between different situations. If you're good at designing webpage, you'd probably also be good at designing pages in a magazine. That's a flex skill. So your know skills and your flex skills. Now, you might have heard these called hard skills and [[Soft Skills]], but there's nothing soft about flex skills. They're so important,
