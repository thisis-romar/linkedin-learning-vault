# Copilot in PowerPoint: From Prompt to Presentation

> Copilot In PowerPoint can help you create, refine, and format presentations. In this course, Microsoft PowerPoint MVP Julie Terberg shows you how to turn your ideas—whether typed as a prompt or drawn from an existing document—into a complete set of well‑structured slides. Learn how to guide Copilot to generate slides tailored to your topic and adjust the text, images, and layout to fit your needs.
