# Automating Your Work with Custom GPTs (No Code Required) (2025)

> A GPT is a custom version of ChatGPT tailored by you to perform a specific task or meet a desired goal under parameters that you define. This course provides several examples of GPTs you can create in minutes to save time and automate your work. Create a GPT to analyze an archive of PDF files, extract text from images, or provide you with career coaching. The possibilities are endless.
