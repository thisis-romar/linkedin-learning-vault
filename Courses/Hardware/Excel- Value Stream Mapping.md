As the name applies, it goes into some depth about the techniques that I've demonstrated here. Also, there is the book, "Learning To See" by Rother and Shook. This takes a look at value stream mapping and it's from a lean process perspective. So, if you do any sort of lean engineering, analysis or process improvement, this book will be exactly what you need to continue that work. And, finally, from a broader operations management standpoint, I cannot recommend, "Matching Supply with Demand", highly enough. The two authors, Cachon and Terwiesch, do a terrific job. Thanks again and I hope that you can find a way to improve the processes in your organization using value stream maps.
